How to use the soundBoard

1. extract the zip file

tutorial for unzipping

2. launch the Soundpad in the extracted folder

3. it should launch an app

4. on the top bar press "files" and preferences

5. go to devices

6. select the checkbox next to "Microphone (Steam streaming Microphone)"

7. Restart win-audio (the button on the bottom)

8. close the pop-up

9. go to youtube

10. search for your sound

11. copy the link to the video with the sound

12. go to https://ytmp3s.nu/ (not sponsored)

13. paste the video link into the text box

14. press convert button

15. download the sound

16. drag the downloaded file into the soundboard

17. double-click the name in the soundboard to play it on you steam microphone
